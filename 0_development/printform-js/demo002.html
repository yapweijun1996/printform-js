This Purchase Order ("PO") constitutes an offer by Apex Construction Ltd. ("Buyer") to purchase the goods and/or services described herein from the addressee ("Seller"). 2. PRICE AND PAYMENT

Unless otherwise specified, the price stated includes all charges for packing, hauling, storage, transportation to the point of delivery, and taxes. Payment will be made according to the terms specified on the face of this PO. If no terms are specified, payment shall be Net 60 days from the date of receipt of a valid and correct invoice or receipt of goods/services, whichever is later. Buyer reserves the right to withhold payment for defective goods or services.

3. DELIVERY

Time is of the essence. Seller shall deliver the goods and perform services on the dates specified. If delivery is delayed, Seller must immediately notify Buyer. Buyer may, at its option, cancel this PO in whole or in part without liability if delivery is not made on time. Seller shall be liable for any additional costs incurred by Buyer due to late delivery, including but not limited to expedited shipping charges and standby costs for construction crews.

4. INSPECTION AND REJECTION

All goods and services are subject to Buyer's final inspection and acceptance at the destination. Payment for goods or services shall not constitute acceptance. Buyer reserves the right to reject any goods or services that do not conform to specifications, drawings, samples, or other descriptions. Rejected goods will be returned to Seller at Seller's risk and expense.

5. WARRANTY

Seller warrants that all goods and services furnished hereunder will be free from defects in design, material, and workmanship; will conform to applicable specifications, drawings, and samples; will be merchantable and fit for their intended purpose; and will be free of any liens or encumbrances. 6. INDEMNIFICATION

Seller agrees to indemnify, defend, and hold harmless Buyer, its officers, directors, employees, agents, and customers from and against any and all claims, liabilities, damages, losses, costs, and expenses (including reasonable attorneys' fees) arising out of or resulting from: (a) any defect in the goods or services; (b) any act or omission of Seller, its agents, or employees; (c) any infringement of third-party intellectual property rights; or (d) Seller's failure to comply with applicable laws and regulations.

7. SAFETY AND JOB SITE RULES

If Seller provides services on Buyer's job site, Seller and its personnel shall comply with all applicable safety laws, regulations, and Buyer's site-specific safety policies. Seller shall ensure that its employees are properly trained and equipped with necessary personal protective equipment (PPE). Buyer reserves the right to remove any of Seller's personnel from the site for safety violations.

Buyer may at any time, by written order, make changes within the general scope of this PO. 9. INSURANCE

Seller shall maintain, at its own expense, comprehensive general liability insurance, workers' compensation insurance, and automobile liability insurance with limits satisfactory to Buyer. Upon request, Seller shall provide Buyer with a certificate of insurance naming Buyer as an additional insured.
